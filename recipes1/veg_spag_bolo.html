Vegan Spaghetti Bolognese

These vegan Spaghetti Bolognese will statisfy your tastebuds, while being cruelty free and easy to make!

For the vegan meat, just use your favorite kind. I like to go with firm tofu crumble and dry spices to marinate.

Ingridients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 1 white/red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • olive oil
  • 400g meat substitute
  • 1/2 tube tomato paste
  • 200ml red wine/dark current juice
  • 1 carrot
  • 500g tomato passata
  • salt
  • pepper
  • fresh basil
  • dry italian herbs

Instructions

  1. Start by cutting up your vegetables in fine cubes and mince the garlic. Then set aside and bring one pot of salted water to boil.
  2. While waiting for the water to start booiling, we begin to fry the meat in a sufficient amount of olive oil.
  3. Add pasta to the boiling water and follow instructions on the packaging. If done earlier than the sauce, strain it but keep some pasta water and set aside.
  4. Add Onions to the meat and let the onion get some colour. Add the carrots and garlic. The garlic mustn't burn.
  5. Incorporate the tomato paste and fry it for a bit to get some roasted aromas.
  6. Now in goes the wine or the juice and let simmer down for a bit, then add in the tomato passata.
  7. Season to taste with the spices and add in some pasta water
  8. Mix up the sauce with the pasta and garnish with the fresh basil and serve warm.
